✦ How Are Lucky Numbers Calculated?

Name Number — The most important number derived from your name. Calculated by adding all the Chaldean values of the letters in your name and reducing to a single digit. It reveals the hidden meaning of your name and your core vibration.


Lucky Number — A name which brings luck and prosperity to you becomes your lucky name. Similarly, your lucky number helps you to become successful in any undertaking. A lucky day of the week which falls on your lucky number or friendly number is more favorable.


Friendly Numbers — Numbers that have harmonious vibrations with your name number. Dates, addresses, phone numbers or any other numbers that reduce to a friendly number are favorable for you.


Enemy Numbers — Numbers that create friction or challenges when they appear in your life. It is advisable to avoid important decisions on dates that reduce to enemy numbers.


Life Path Number — Derived by reducing your full date of birth (day + month + year) to a single digit. It represents your innate traits and the path you are destined to walk in this lifetime.


Personal Year Number — Changes every year. Calculated by adding your birth day + birth month + current year. It guides the theme and energy of your current year.
