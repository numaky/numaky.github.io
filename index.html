✦ How Are Lucky Numbers Calculated?

Life Path Number — The most important number in numerology. Derived by reducing your full date of birth (day + month + year) to a single digit (or master number 11, 22, 33). It represents your innate traits, the challenges you will face, and the opportunities available.


Lucky Number — Calculated by adding your Life Path with your Name Number (Chaldean method). This is your most fortunate number for daily decisions.


Destiny Number (Expression) — The sum of all letters of your full birth name, reduced to a single digit. It represents your natural talents and what you are destined to achieve.


Soul Urge Number — Calculated only from the vowels in your full name. Reveals your inner self, deepest desires, and what truly motivates you.


Inner Dream Number — Calculated from the consonants of your name. Represents your unconscious aspirations, your secret self.


Personal Year Number — Changes every January. Calculated by adding your birth month + birth day + current year. Guides the theme and energy of your current year.

✦ For entertainment & spiritual reflection purposes. Numbers are symbolic guides, not guarantees. ✦
